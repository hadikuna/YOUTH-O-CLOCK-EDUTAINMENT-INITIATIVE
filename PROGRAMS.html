PROGRAMS

YOUTH-O'CLOCK EDUTAINMENT INITIATIVE is an engaging and innovative initiative that inspires and prepares YOUTHS for the future.

We also annually organise an incredibly impactful programme that aims at discussing critical issues providing tools and resources needed to make a lasting impact in our communities and beyond.

After a thorough review and critical analysis, we also recognize ( through awards) some important figures that contribute immensely to the humanity.

YEI also has an academy that teaches and motivates students from different levels on English and Communication Skills, Career guidance, Public speaking and Capacity building.




It was founded in 2021 and had its maiden annual programme in 2023 with the theme "Recollecting the Past and Redesigning the Future" under the auspices of its Founder and Executive Director- Muhammad Kabir Muntaka.
